About Us

Vazquez Tours & Travel Ltd is a bespoke safari company based in Kigali - Rwanda, which specializes in providing lifetime safari experiences in East Africa. There is no better way to experience East Africa than to be on a luxury safari trip with the ultimate travel experts. Our destinations and experiences are incredibly unique, and have a strong focus on nature. From the Volcanic Virunga Mountains in Rwanda to the famous magical Kenya, the Land of Kilimanjaro ‘Tanzania’, and Uganda ‘the pearl of Africa’; East Africa is a region with incredible diversity and some of the world’s most beautiful wild places. You will encounter the endangered mountain gorillas in their natural habitat at Virunga National Park, the famous tree-climbing lions of Ishasha in Uganda, and the annual great migrations from Kenya’s legendary Masai Mara across the border to Tanzania’s Serengeti National Park. Our motto is that every guest on safari with Vazquez Tours & Travel Ltd will experience a unique and a lifetime experience in one of Africa’s most beautiful and varied landscapes. Between us, our team has visited all the destinations that we sell. Our destinations specialists have unrivalled knowledge in creating tailor-made safaris, tours and wildlife journeys for both individuals and small groups.
